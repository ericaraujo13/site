    Considering Law No. 13,709, dated August 14, 2018 – General Data Protection Law (LGPD), which regulates the processing of personal data, including in digital means, by natural or legal persons, public or private; Considering that LGPD aims to protect fundamental rights of

    freedom and privacy, as well as the free development of the personality of natural persons; Considering that LGPD is grounded in human rights, free development of personality, dignity,

    and the exercise of citizenship by natural persons, respecting privacy, inviolability of intimacy, honor, and image, freedom of expression, information, communication, and opinion, all as

    provided and guaranteed by the Federal Constitution; PluralSign establishes its "Privacy and Personal Data Protection Policies" with the purpose of presenting the main data processing activities, such as: access, storage, archiving, classification, collection, communication, control, dissemination, deletion, modification, processing, sharing, reproduction, transmission, transfer, and use. The purpose of this document is to provide data subjects with clear and accurate information about these processing activities, ensuring compliance with regulations and the implementation of systems, tools, and services capable of protecting and monitoring the processing of personal data. PluralSign assumes the role of "CONTROLLER," that is, the

    entity collecting personal data and making decisions regarding the entire data processing process, defining its purpose and storage period. "PERSONAL DATA," according to LGPD, is defined as any information related to an identified or identifiable natural person (a natural

    person who can be identified by reasonably likely means to be used). Personal data includes, directly or indirectly, all individuals, including (but not limited to) employees and consumer customers.
