Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) - Digital Diploma Signature API

A digital diploma signature is a secure technological method that replaces traditional manual signatures on printed diplomas. It involves applying a unique and encrypted electronic signature, ensuring the authenticity and integrity of the diploma.

Our API simplifies the diploma issuance process by automating the generation and digital signature of diplomas. The university provides student details and diploma requirements, and the API creates the digital diploma, applies the digital signature, and delivers it electronically to the student.

Digital diplomas are delivered to students electronically, usually via email or through a secure access platform. Students can download the diploma, share it online, and even print it if they choose to.

Our API uses advanced encryption to protect the digital signature and diploma data. Encryption ensures that information cannot be altered or falsified, providing an exceptional level of security.
